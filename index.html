Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2024

At RunMemo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data:

  1. No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or share personal data.
  2. Local Storage: Your notes are stored entirely on your personal device(s).
  3. User Control: Deleting the app permanently erases your notes.
  4. Changes to Privacy Policy: Updates will be communicated via the app or website.

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@abbond.com.
